How can we help you?

Find answers to frequently asked questions and support for our services.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you can track it through the 'My Orders' section in your account.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, cancellations are not possible.
